## 2. The research angles (how each pack was scoped)

The method, both times: **study the raw export first, hand-compute expected values independently (python over the TSV), only then design** ‚Äî the engine must reproduce numbers derived without it.

**Pricing** ‚Äî column census of `ReportLogistic20260618-IVO.xls` (184 columns) on the 62 unallocated rows:
- `S.Dif` / `S.Fob dif` populated **62/62** ‚Üí differentials are the reliable "price level" (USc/lb vs NY KC). `S. Price` only 29/62 (rest = price-to-be-fixed); units mixed USC/LB √ó55, USD/KG √ó4, USD/MT √ó3 ‚Üí normalization needed (1 USc/lb = 22.046226 USD/MT).
- `S.Term` mixed (FOB/CIF/DAP‚Ä¶) ‚Äî why contract dif ‚âÝ FOB dif ‚Üí **user decision: always show both, no headline**.
- Purchase-side price fields ALL ZERO on unallocated rows ‚Üí cost basis impossible from this export (declared out of scope; agent declines).
- Golden BASE FILE fixture and demo seed carry no prices ‚Üí validation constants hand-computed from the raw TSV; demo seed enriched by joining the real export on (saleCtr, month).

**Client/shipment** ‚Äî status + coverage census:
- The export is **pre-filtered to '6-Sales Unallocated'** (62/62) ‚Äî no shipped rows exist, so "shipment analytics" = *booking state of the forward book*, not voyage tracking.
- 100%-covered fields: `S.City`, `S.CodCountry`, `Payment term`, `Trader`; `S.Cert` 20/62 (EUDR flags).
- Booking-stage fields partial by nature: PreshipID 18, vessel/ETD 13, ETA 10 ‚Äî **and `'0'` is the empty marker** on booking fields (caught when a naive count said 61/61 booked).
- The header repeats Vessel/ETD/ETA/POD/TransType for a second transport leg that is entirely empty ‚Üí first-match column lookup is correct.
- Split contracts ride one sailing on several bookings (SSKE-103502: two preship IDs, one vessel) ‚Üí merge joins IDs with ' / ', keeps earliest dates. This recovered the 17th booked contract during TDD.
